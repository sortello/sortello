Prioritize your lists, the simple way.
Using a binary tree based algorithm, Sortello will help you prioritize the cards of a Trello list, proposing simple 1vs1 choices
Try Sortello!
HOW IT WORKS
Improve your meetings, improve your time. With Sortello everyone can participate in prioritizing, bringing pragmatic solutions.
STEP 1
Install the Sortello Chrome Extension from the Chrome Web Store
STEP 2
Go to your Trello board, choose the column to be prioritized and click on the sortello's button
STEP 3
Sortello compares the cards in order to facilitate prioritization
STEP 4
Check the result and send the new sorted list to Trello
WHY IT WORKS
Deciding which one out of two tasks has higher priority, is easier than having to sort a list of n tasks at once.
Sortello simplifies the prioritization process by leveraging binary tree sort efficiency, with an added balancing algorithm.
You can keep focus on simple choices while Sortello works to make the process as short as possible.
We do not store any data server-side. Sortello uses cookies only to enable Trello authentication and anonimously track visits to the page. Read more here.
