Privacy Statement: How we use Cookies

Cookies are very small text files that are stored on your computer when you visit some websites.
We do not use cookies on Sortello.com, but there are a few third-party cookies:
  • Google Analytics - we use this service only to track access to Sortello, we use anonymized information.
  • Trello - In order to use Sortello.com you need to identify with your Trello account.
You can disable any cookies already stored on your computer, but disabling Trello from setting cookies may stop Sortello from functioning properly.

Functional Cookies - This Website Will:

There are no Targeting Cookies on Sortello.com - This website will not:
